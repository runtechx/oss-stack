# Statement

**RunTech** follows an **“Open First”** approach, prioritizing the adoption, promotion, and contribution to **Open Source software** and **open standards** across all technology decisions.

We believe open source provides greater transparency, auditability, and sustainability, reducing vendor lock-in while increasing control over critical systems and data. This philosophy enables the development of more flexible, secure, and business-aligned infrastructures.

## Open First Policy

At **RunTech**, technology adoption is based on a simple, conscious, and sustainable model:

1. **Prioritize Open Source Software**
   Open source is the default choice whenever a technically viable solution exists. We prioritize self-hosted platforms and actively encourage their use, improvement, and contribution back to the community. This approach strengthens technological independence, operational transparency, and long-term adaptability.

2. **Adopt Proprietary Solutions Only When Necessary**
   When no suitable open source alternative meets the required technical or operational needs, we carefully adopt proprietary or SaaS solutions. In such cases, preference is given to vendors aligned with open standards, security best practices, privacy, and interoperability. All decisions are periodically reviewed to evaluate future migration opportunities toward open source alternatives.

## Operational Principles

Our infrastructure and technological processes are guided by the following principles:

* **Open First by Design** – Open source is the starting point for every technology decision.
* **Automation** – Infrastructure and services managed as code whenever possible.
* **Security** – Security controls and best practices implemented from the ground up (*security by design*).
* **Scalability** – Solutions designed for growth, evolution, and high availability.
* **Observability** – Continuous monitoring, metrics, and visibility across systems and services.

## Contributions

We believe the open source ecosystem evolves through collaboration and knowledge sharing.

If you would like to suggest new technologies, improvements, or alternatives to the solutions currently adopted, we invite you to open an *issue* in this repository.

All contributions are welcome, especially those that:

* Present open source alternatives aligned with our principles.
* Include technical justification and/or comparisons with existing solutions.
* Help improve infrastructure efficiency, security, automation, or scalability.

If this project has been useful to you, consider:

* Leaving a ⭐ on the repository
* Following the organization to stay updated on new features, stacks, and open source automations
* Sharing the project with other technology professionals and open source communities

Your participation helps strengthen a more open, accessible, and collaborative technology ecosystem.
